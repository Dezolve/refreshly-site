Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2026

Refreshly is designed to work without requiring an account. Hydration entries and app preferences are stored on your device, and we do not sell personal information.

Information we collect

  • Hydration and preference details you enter, such as goals, units, and reminder settings.
  • Notification permission status if you choose to enable reminders.

How data is stored

Refreshly stores hydration history and settings locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, local data may be removed based on your device settings.

Data sharing

Refreshly does not sell personal information and does not share hydration logs with third parties.

Analytics and crash reporting

If analytics or crash reporting is added in the future, it will be used only to improve reliability and performance, and will not include personal hydration entries.

Children's privacy

Refreshly is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will appear on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@refreshly.app.
