Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-10

Summary: Refreshly is designed to work without requiring an account. Your hydration data is stored on your device. We do not sell your personal information.

What information Refreshly collects

  • Hydration and preference data you enter (for example: daily goal, units, reminder preferences, and logged intake).
  • Notification permissions if you choose to enable reminders.

How your data is stored

Refreshly stores your hydration history and settings locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data may be removed depending on your device settings.

Data sharing

Refreshly does not sell your personal information. We do not share your hydration logs with third parties.

Analytics and crash reporting (optional)

If we use analytics or crash reporting in the future, it will be for improving app stability and performance. Any such data would be limited to technical information (for example: app version, device type, and crash logs) and would not include your hydration entries.

If you know you are not using analytics/crash reporting, you may remove this section.

Children’s privacy

Refreshly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: support@refreshly.app
