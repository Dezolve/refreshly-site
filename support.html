Support

Need help with Refreshly? Reach us directly.

Email support@refreshly.app and include device model, iOS version, app version, and what happened.

Recommended troubleshooting details

  • Device model and current iOS version.
  • Refreshly app version from App Store settings.
  • Expected behavior versus actual behavior.
  • Whether reminders are enabled and what schedule is configured.

Common questions

How do I change my daily goal?
Open Settings, then adjust Goal mode and values.

How can I modify reminders?
Go to Settings and update reminder toggles and times.

Is my hydration data cloud synced?
By default, data is stored locally on your device.
